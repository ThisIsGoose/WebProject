About

As a life-long metalhead and appreciator of music, I needed a space to share my thoughts and opinions on the world of metal. I created this website to provide a platform, and I hope that you will enjoy reading my takes and listening along as you form your own personal opinions.

This is not a website for objective reviews of the albums posted, but I try my best to minimize any biases I have to deliver the best possible reviews for the albums I've been listening to. If you wish to share your own opinions, feel free to contact me via the Contact page.
